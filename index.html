How it works

Three steps. Your effort decides everything.

1

Set your goal & pledge

Choose a goal — run 15 miles a week, hit 10k steps a day — and pledge $10–$500. We save your card, but charge nothing up front.

2

Your wearable verifies

We read the activity straight from Apple Health, Android Health Connect, or Strava. Only real sensor data counts — no photos, no honor system.

3

Succeed, or give back

Hit your goal and you pay nothing. Fall short and your pledge is donated to the charity you picked — routed directly, never held by us.

Built for three sides

Personal accountability, corporate wellness, and charitable impact — in one loop.

🏃 Individuals

Stake solo or with friends. A commitment contract that actually keeps you consistent — and turns a missed week into a gift to a cause you care about.

🏢 Companies

A CSR + wellness tool. Match employees' stakes so every success becomes a company donation — real engagement, measurable impact, clean tax reporting.

❤️ Charities

A high-intent donor pipeline, plus a training companion for runners chasing the milestones behind their marathon-bib fundraising.

Not a betting app.

There's no prize pool and nothing to win. You only ever risk your own money against your own goal, and every forfeit goes to a verified charity — never to another user, and never to us. It's a commitment device, not a wager.

Honest about fees

You always see exactly what you pledge.

If you fall short, your pledge is what's charged — and it's inclusive of fees. A small platform fee (3.5–5%) plus payment processing come out of the pledge, and the charity receives the remainder. We'll always show this before you commit: "your $50 pledge includes ~$4 in fees."

Corporate matches are donated in full, with no platform fee. Forfeited stakes are a structural philanthropic processing fee, not a personal tax-deductible donation.
